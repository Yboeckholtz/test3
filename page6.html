PROCESS

REFERENCES

To start of with the process we discussed and analyzed references and practical examples of multidisciplinary working and thinking, from the past and present. By looking for those references we looked for cases, projects, works, ideas, person and trends that inspired us and we asked ourselves the question why those particular references impressed us. Based on the found references in the context of multidisciplinarity on the one hand and installation art on the other, we discussed which images had the greatest potential according to us and would contribute most in our multidisciplinary creative process. We subdivided them into 3 categories: Multidisciplinarity, Art installations and Covid-19.
